Welcome To Our Website

The KIPRA Portal is an online platform developed to provide information and services related to agriculture. It aims to bridge the gap between farmers and various stakeholders in the agricultural ecosystem. The portal offers a range of features and tools to support farmers in making informed decisions and improving their agricultural practices for farmers to connect with agricultural experts, marketplaces, and weather updates

Objective

  • Farmers can get real-time market prices for their produce, helping them make better decisions about when and where to sell.
  • The portal provides weather forecasts and alerts, enabling farmers to plan their farming activities accordingly.
  • Farmers can receive expert advice and guidance on various aspects of agriculture, such as crop selection, pest and disease management, and soil health.
